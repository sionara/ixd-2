By Jenn Evans
20 Community followers
Follow
Details
Join Toronto People Analytics for an inaugural group meeting downtown TO for an evening of learning and networking!
What to expect
We will kick off our periodic meet-ups with a first event hosted at Deloitte with a panel of People Analytics leaders from a variety of industries to discuss the biggest challenges and opportunities facing the People Analytics community today. Afterwards, we will spend time networking with other professionals in the field. We would love to have you there.
Please sign up to confirm attendance by April 15th, 2024, @ 5:00 PM, as the space is limited.
